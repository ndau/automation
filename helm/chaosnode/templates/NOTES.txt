Your chaosnode is accessible at any of the IP addresses associated with your cluster.

To get the IP addresses of the nodes in your cluster:
```
kubectl get nodes -o jsonpath='{.items[*].status.addresses[?(@.type=="ExternalIP")].address}' | tr " " "\n"
```

To get the specific ports assigned on each node.
echo "P2P port: $(kubectl get service --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -o jsonpath="{.spec.ports[?(@.name==\"p2p\")].nodePort}" {{ template "chaos.fullname" . }}-tendermint-service)"
echo "RPC port: $(kubectl get service --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -o jsonpath="{.spec.ports[?(@.name==\"rpc\")].nodePort}" {{ template "chaos.fullname" . }}-tendermint-service)"

On minikube:

```
minikube service  --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} {{ template "chaos.fullname" . }}-tendermint-service --url
```
